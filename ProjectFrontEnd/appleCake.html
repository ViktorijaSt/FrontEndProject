I'm not a chef, not imagine myself also like chef, but baking and cooking is the just must do for me.
My favorite recipe is my mother apple cake. Like now, I'm feeling that aroma of cinnamon and appels in our house. Beautiful memoris of mine from my past. Has been a family favorite dessert for many years.

Joking - that's my way. But heey.. you should try also.


RECIPE

Apple Cake

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla sugar
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • pinch of ground of clove
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 cups apples - peeled, cored and diced
  • one cup walnuts
  • one cup dates

    • Directions

    Preheat an oven to 200 degrees C. Put oil on a baking pan or baking paper.
    Combine flour, sugar, vegetable oil, salt, baking soda, vanilla, clove and cinnamon in a large bowl. Mix well.
    Stir in the chopped apples, dates and walnuts until just combined. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 1 hour.


    Be careful, this cake will disappear very fast. I'm garanting you. (Yaaay).
